Money-Saving Tips

Off-Season Savings
Consider getting married during off-peak months (January-March or November-December) to save 15-30% on venue and vendor costs.
Daytime Wedding
Morning or afternoon receptions can be 30-50% less expensive than evening events, with lower venue rental and catering costs.
Trim the Guest List
Reducing your guest list by just 10 people can save $1,000-$3,000 depending on your venue and catering choices.
Photography Package
Book photography for fewer hours (ceremony + reception only) instead of full-day coverage to save $500-$1,500.
DJ vs Live Band
A professional DJ typically costs $1,000-$2,500 compared to $3,000-$10,000 for a live band, with similar dance floor results.
In-Season Flowers
Choose flowers that are in season for your wedding date to reduce floral costs by 20-40% compared to out-of-season blooms.
