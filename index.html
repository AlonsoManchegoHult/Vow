About Vow

Where wedding dreams meet innovative planning

Making Wedding Dreams Come True

Born from a passion for perfect weddings, Vow was founded in 2020 by wedding enthusiasts who understood the challenges of modern wedding planning. We saw couples struggling with scattered inspiration, difficult planner discovery, and overwhelming coordination.

Our Pinterest-inspired approach revolutionizes wedding planning by bringing everything together in one beautiful, intuitive platform. We're not just a service; we're your wedding planning partner, dedicated to making your journey as joyful as the destination.
