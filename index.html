Our Story

Vow was founded with a simple mission: to make wedding planning a joyful experience rather than a stressful one. Our founder, Sarah Johnson, experienced firsthand how overwhelming the wedding planning process can be when planning her own wedding in 2015.

After working with multiple vendors and struggling to coordinate all the details, she realized there was a need for a service that could connect couples with vetted, professional wedding planners who could handle every aspect of the planning process.

Today, Vow has helped over 5,000 couples create their dream weddings, with a team of 50+ expert planners across the country. We believe that every couple deserves a wedding day that reflects their unique love story, without the stress that often comes with planning it.
